Description:
Tứ thư ước giải is a vernacular Vietnamese commentary on the Confucian Four Books written in the Nôm script. The full work is made up of 5 books, all complete with the exception of volumes 3 – 9. The first book contains the preface and the table of contents alongside volumes 1 – 2. The translator is unknown, but this edition and the preface is complied by Lê Quý Đôn (黎貴惇; 1726 – 1784).
